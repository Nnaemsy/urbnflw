Pinned Best Night Skating Routes in London?

Posted 2 hours ago in Skating Spots
SkateQueen

Pro Skater

Looking for recommendations on safe and well-lit routes for night skating in London. I've been exploring different areas but would love to hear about your favorite spots!

So far I've tried:

  • Hyde Park: Good lighting but closes early sometimes.
  • South Bank: Great atmosphere, but can get crowded, especially on weekends.
  • Olympic Park (Stratford): Excellent smooth surfaces, feels quite safe.

Any other suggestions? Particularly interested in routes that are:

  • Well-lit
  • Smooth surfaces (minimal cracks/cobbles!)
  • Good safety record / generally feels safe
  • Accessible by public transport

Cheers!

Victoria Park has great lighting and a dedicated cycle path that's perfect for skating. They've also got security patrols until around 11pm most nights which is reassuring. Pretty easy to get to as well.

Seconding Victoria Park! Also, the area around Canary Wharf after office hours is surprisingly good - wide pavements, well lit, and usually empty late at night. Just watch out for the odd security guard telling you to move on, but mostly they're chill.
